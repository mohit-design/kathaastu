About The Book

Locked Out of Life is a contemporary novel about loss, love and what follows when you try to move on from it. Set over the course of a New Year's Eve, the story follows Jay as he searches for a missing key and the turtle keyring his late wife once gifted him. Along the way, he meets strangers carrying questions, loneliness, grief, hope and unfinished journeys. Told through interconnected perspectives, the novel explores how ordinary lives unknowingly change one another. Perfect for readers who enjoy emotionally driven, character-focused fiction.
